# Impact Assessment

## Operational Impact

- Employees could not access vacation package information
- Customers experienced website downtime
- Business operations and customer service were disrupted

## Security Impact

- Web server availability was reduced
- Network resources were overwhelmed
- Legitimate traffic was delayed or dropped

## Potential Consequences

- Financial loss due to service interruption
- Reduced customer trust
- Increased operational downtime
- Potential future attacks if protections are not improved
